What is Poetry?

Welcome to Poignant Poetry, your one stop shop for all things poetic. Poetry is a form of literature that expresses the narrator's emotions and feelings. There are four types of poems: lyric poems, narrative poems, humorous poems, and concrete poems. Lyric poems are short poems that depict a short period of time. It gives the reader imagery, usually expressing the poet's past experiences or wishes. Most of our poems on this website are lyric poems.

Poetry is often known to help people relax. It can stir deeply rooted emotions, letting the reader have a sense of their encounters. We hope you like this set of poetry that will help you relax, understand, and believe.
